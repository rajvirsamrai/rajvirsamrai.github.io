Mosaic
August 9th, 2020

An image abstraction tool to make pixel art. Convert high resolution images to lower resolutions while limiting the colour palette.

Based on the algorithm and ideas presented in "Computers & Graphics, ISSN: 0097-8493, Vol: 37, Issue: 5, Page: 333-347"
